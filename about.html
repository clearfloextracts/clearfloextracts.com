Our story

Experience you can
actually rely on.

ClearFlo Extracts was founded by Stuart Bentley — a commercial maintenance and compliance specialist with close to a decade of focused industry experience across ventilation hygiene, fire compliance, building trades and facilities management.

Stuart has worked across some of the most operationally demanding commercial environments in the country, including Canary Wharf, where compliance standards, permit requirements and documentation expectations are as rigorous as they come.

ClearFlo operates the way a good contractor should — you speak to Stuart, Stuart turns up, Stuart does the work. No hand-offs, no surprises, no corners cut.
