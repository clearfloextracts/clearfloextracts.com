Compliance

Why TR19 compliance matters

TR19 — the UK standard

TR19 sets the benchmark for kitchen extract cleaning. Every ClearFlo clean delivers a full photographic TR19 grease report — not just a receipt that says the job was done.

Your insurance may not pay out

Grease build-up is a leading cause of commercial kitchen fires. If your extraction isn't cleaned to TR19 standard, your insurer may refuse a fire claim. Don't find out the hard way.

Fire damper compliance

Fire dampers must be tested and serviced annually to BS 9999 standards. Failed or untested dampers are a serious life-safety risk and a regulatory liability. We install, service and certify.

RAMS-led delivery

All works are delivered with full risk assessments and method statements. We operate confidently within permit-to-work environments — having spent years on the most controlled commercial sites in London.
